In 2023, Reynolds Dryline celebrated a remarkable milestone — 60 years of delivering exceptional building finishes across the United Kingdom. What began as a small, family-run business in Leicestershire has grown into one of the most respected specialist subcontractors in the country.

## Our Founding Story

L Reynolds (Building Systems) Ltd was founded in 1963 by a craftsman with a passion for quality and a determination to build something lasting. From the very beginning, the company was built on the principles of reliability, quality, and integrity — values that remain at the heart of everything we do today.

## Six Decades of Growth

Over the past six decades, we have grown from a small local contractor to a nationally recognised specialist subcontractor, delivering projects from Sheffield to London and beyond. Our portfolio spans residential, commercial, industrial, and leisure sectors, with project values ranging from hundreds of thousands to tens of millions of pounds.

## Our People

The most important factor in our success has always been our people. We are proud to employ some of the most skilled and dedicated professionals in the industry, many of whom have been with the company for decades. Their expertise, commitment, and passion for quality are what set Reynolds Dryline apart.

## Looking to the Future

As we look ahead, we remain committed to the same principles that have guided us for 60 years. We will continue to invest in our people, embrace new technologies and techniques, and build on the relationships that have made Reynolds Dryline the trusted partner of choice for the UK's leading contractors.

Thank you to everyone who has been part of our journey — our clients, our partners, and most importantly, our team.
