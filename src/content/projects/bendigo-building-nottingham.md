The Bendigo Building is a significant residential development in the heart of Nottingham city centre, comprising hundreds of high-quality apartments. Reynolds Dryline has been appointed by main contractor Graham to deliver the complete internal building finishes package.

## Our Scope of Works

Our scope on this project includes:

- **Metal stud partitioning** throughout all residential floors and common areas
- **Suspended ceiling systems** in common areas, corridors, and apartments
- **Structural Framing Systems (SFS)** for the external envelope
- **Acoustic and fire-rated systems** to meet the project's demanding performance requirements

## Challenges & Solutions

The Bendigo Building presented several technical challenges, including complex acoustic requirements for the residential units and tight programme constraints. Our experienced team worked closely with the main contractor and design team to develop practical solutions that met all performance targets without impacting the programme.

## Delivery

Reynolds Dryline is proud to be delivering this project to the highest standards, working collaboratively with Graham and the wider project team to ensure a successful outcome.
